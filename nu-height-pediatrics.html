NU HEIGHT PEDIATRICS - Clifton, NJ

Our clients decided to expand their pediatricians office space and requested our services. Our team was asked to renovate an existing building and design a facility that would contain solely for children and young adults. They wanted a facility that was fun for children and welcoming to parents and caregivers.

We created an environment full of imagination, color, and positive distractions. It is a facility that contains not only top-of-the-line equipment, but also a fun space. There are 13 exam rooms, which are organized along a looping corridor creating a simple circulation path: a waiting room, an exam room, a check-in/check-out area and centralized nurses’ stations.
Status: Completed, 2017.
